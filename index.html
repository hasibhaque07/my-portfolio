About Me

I’m Hasib and I enjoy turning ideas into products. I work mainly with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) and Next.js, so I can take a project from the first sketch to a working web app.

I like building SEO-friendly web apps with Next.js, while keeping the backend scalable and use tools like Google Analytics and Tag Manager to make sure decisions are backed by data. I am exploring the world of design as well, creating brand identities and logos.

Startups move quickly, and that pace excites me. I enjoy being part of that energy.
